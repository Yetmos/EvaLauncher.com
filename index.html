Product summary

EvaLauncher is for Android users who want more than a utility shell.

It combines a visual mascot layer with a launcher workflow that stays practical: quick entry points, searchable content, folder organization, and a settings surface that exposes permission-sensitive behavior instead of hiding it.

Interaction model

Live2D home, floating actions, bottom-sheet drawer, and configurable overlay mode.

Core utilities

Search, launch history, folders, system shortcuts, language access, and privacy handoff.

Design direction

Soft contrast, high legibility, motion-first presence, and a distinct character silhouette.
